Services

Consulation and Training

We offer consulatationary and training services in all areas of the sector. With over 10 years of expereince, we have vast knowledge and expereince in all the value chains of crops produced in Nigeia. Our previous clients includes individuals, students, NGOs, private companies, govenment organizations etc.

Research and Development

We are commited to breeding and developement of new crop varieties that have excellent qaulities and can withstand biotic and abiotic stress posed by environment.

Extention Services

We embark on new innovation and technological dissemination for farmers to help improve thier crop yeilds, thereby increasing quality income. Our activities also helps to stengthen the resilence of the farmers to challenges like climate, pest and diseases attack that may pose a threat to crops.

Agric Business

We are active in all areas of the agriculture value chain we carry out investigations in both the grains and seed value chain, bringing in high standards of quality starting from production down to the transportation to the final consumer.
