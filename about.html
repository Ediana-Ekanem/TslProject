About Tecni Seeds

TECNI SEEDS started production of seeds since 2009 and was registered with CACA and the National Agricutural seed council in 2011. Tecni seeds has it's Head Office at 66 Hadeja Road Kano and have several branches/outlets in different parts of Nigeria. This places include and not limited to Bauchi, Gombe, Jigawa, Kaduna, Sokoto, Ibadan, Akure, Lagos. We produce and market both arable and vegetable seeds of different crops and varieties. We offer extention services and consulting services to fermers especially women and help them in establishing of thier own farms.
Tecni Seeds have consultand breeders working with us from within and outside the country. We have access to Breeder Seeds and Foundation Seeds from Research Institudes depending on the crop in question.

Available seeds for sale

We produce and market both arable and vegetable seeds of different crops and varieties which include Maize, Rice, Sorghum, Groundnut, Millet, Sesame, Cowpea, Soybean and vegetable seeds we have Tomato, Pepper, Water melon, Lettuce, Cabbage, Carrot just to mention a few.
